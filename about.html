ABOUT US

We are a team of High-Schoolers (one college graduate) who make games. The team was founded by current CEO, John Fiore, and was Co-Founded by the current COO, Nick Armes. Our games mainly release for PC, but we hope to release VR titles as well. We have previously released ONE GAME, but it is no longer on the market, and we don't talk about it. Our future titles use the Unity Game Engine, which uses the C# Programming Language.
